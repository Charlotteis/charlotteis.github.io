I became obsessed with the number eight. The number eight was a source of unhappiness, of despair, but sadly of comfort. If it's possible to have a love-hate relationship with a number, this it it. This is an illustration of my experience with OCD and the number eight, everything below takes place in about five minutes, repeatedly throughout a normal day.
